Hobbies

In the year of 2020, I went to 35 different hikes across the state of Washington. I enjoy the beauty of the nature, and the walk. My goal is to hike up the Mt. Everest before I turn 30. Hiking is my way to understand the environment, and self reflect on who I am.

I travelled to 12 different countries in 4 years. From travelling, I enjoy learning about new culture, capturing images, and meeting new people. While travelling, I go to the museums, landmarks, and interact with the locals to learn more about their culture.

I am passionate about learning in all different forms. Sometimes, I learn new skills and concept by reading a book, I learn through social media platforms, and even enjoy learning from the people.

Introduction

Hi I'm Nicolas. Interaction Designer based in Seattle, WA, currently designing for Super Purposes™ web application. Before working for Super Purposes™, I was a student at Indiana University majoring in Informatics, and Human Computer Interaction.

My interest in Interaction design began when I was developing a website for University organization (Healthcare Journey IU). Since then, I learned how to conduct different user research, document user's goals/insights, and design for all people in both classroom environment, and in corporate environment.

My goal is influence and empower teams, organizations, and people through design. I aim to use my detailed orientedness and creative thinking skills to bring unforgettable experiences to the users.
