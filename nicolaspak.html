Who I am

Nice to meet you!

I'm a Product Designer, Content Strategist and Researcher, who recently graduated from Indiana University Bloomington. I majored in Informatics and minored in Human Computer Interaction. I also specialized in Computer Science, Machine Learning and Product Management.

My life as an interaction designer started in the summer of 2018, when I started a PM internship at Nube9, a start up company in Seattle. Throughout the internship, I worked closely with the software engineers, and designers to help create a roadmap for the product and for the success. From the Internship, I began tasting my love and passion in design, and began investing my time to design work.

Since the fall of 2018, I am a part of Indiana University Healthcare Journey IU HCI team. IU Healthcare Journey is a healthcare Informatics research team, helping mothers in pregancy in rural Indiana counties where healthcare system is not easily accessible. Throughout my involvement, I conducted a research and interviews throught the mothers in Indiana, brainstormed the best product for resources accessibility and designed/developed a website for the mothers who are looking for healthcare resources. In the fall of 2019, I established a UX Design and Research club at Indiana University, to help all students to fulfill their interest and gain their skills as a UX researcher and UX designer.

I was born in South Korea and I read, write and speak 3 fluent languages. Korean, English and French. My favorite hobby is traveling, bike riding, and hiking. In my freetime, I travel to places I have never been to spend time documenting videos, and writing reviews.

VICI Transport iOS Application

In 21st century, there are many people (especially young adults) who are very interested renting or purchasing a luxury car as a hobby. However, not all luxury cars are easily accessible or cheap. VICI Transport is a booking application that is designed for convenient luxury car booking system. Users do not have to call or visit the car rental agency. Users are able to see the closest location available to them and book the car.

Throughout this project, I was responsible for managing each team members on each sprint with featured list using Agile/Scrum. In addition, this project was designed with Adobe XD for the high fidelity mockup and developed with Swift/XCode and SQL.
