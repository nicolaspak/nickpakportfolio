Literature Review & Context

"The customer wants a new, high class cars to represent a chance of pace in their life or to show that they've reached a personal milestone" (Manoukian).

"Some people gravitate towards luxury brands as a way to boost their self-esteem" (Manoukian).

"Some luxury car brands simply have a better build quality than their competitors" (Manoukian).


The way people access and experience luxury vehicle is about to change.


The literature review from Limelight by Julia Manoukian introduced the ideas of psychological facts of luxury vehicle buyers. Throughout the article, Manoukian mentioned couple different psychological facts of luxury vehicle buyers. According to the article, buyers purchase luxury vehicle experience for self-esteem boost, build quality experience and for status symbol. These psychological factor makes buyers want to purchase luxury vehicles but not many are accessble due to price and accessibility (location).

User Interviews

In order to understand user's experience with the luxury vehicles and their interest, we conducted an interview to 10 different people from all different demographics. The Interview was conducted in different locations around Indiana University campus and interviewers were aged from 19 - 70 with mixed gender. The interviews took about 10 minutes each and as the interview progressed, each of our team member documented people's insights and experience.

Interview Questions

Do you prefer selecting luxury cars when you rent a car?

How do you usually rent a car?

Do you ever use mobile app to book a car, if so which one and how is your experience?

How satisfied with the services offered by the current car booking application?

Tell me about your experience with the cars you booked. What were the pros and cons?

How was the car condition like when you booked and used the car?

Tell me about any negative experiences you had with car reservations if you have any.

Key Findings

Below are the key findings from 10 people I interviewed.

I prefer renting luxury cars over just any car because all the high quality service I get to experience that I can't experience in other regular cars - Nishant, 22 year old

I normally rent a car through car rental agencies like AVIS, Hertz and Enterprise. I booked them through a phone call or booking.com - John, 40 year old

I use booking.com or just visit the car rental sites at the airport or at the city I'm visiting. - Natalia, 56 year old

When I book through Turo, the service is usually good but there was one time I booked a car through Turo and the car was not clean. Since Turo is an app where private owners let you rent the car, it's hard to trust their service sometimes. - Michael, 30 year old

The pro was that the services offered wide variety of prices, locations and different types of cars. The con was the fact that some cars was not clean, and it is only offered by the private vehicle owners - Ernie: 63 year old

The negative condition I had was the overall owner policy. Turo only allows you to drop off the vehicle at the same location you rented. - Daniel: 29 year old

Personas & Case Studies

In order to understand people's frustration and common problems with convenient booking, we wanted to create a Persona to point out common frustrations and goals for people from different demographics. Also, to better understand features and functionality offered by this new service, we created a persona to understand the importance of this application and how convenient this app can be to many users from different demographics (age, gender, ethnicity etc)

Persona 1

Name: Alvaro

Age: 28

Occupation: Data Analyst, Nightclub DJ

Hobby: Soccer

Bio: Alvaro is a Data Analyst and a Nightclub DJ who loves socializing, and to adventure. In his free time, Alvaro spends time travelling to new places around the world. One of his favorite memory was travelling to Sorrento, Italy with his girl friend and roaming around Central Italy with Ferrari.

Frustration & Cases While Alvaro was travelling in Spain with his girl friend, Alvaro had to rent a car to get from Madrid to Barcelona. However, his arrived at the Madrid airport late and could not find alternative ways to get there. He found out that the nearest car rental agency is 30km away and found out it was closed. As a result, Alvaro had to spend the night uncomfortably at the airport and take a subway train to the nearest rental agency.

Goal: Alvaro is looking for ways to select cities and book the cars in advance. Alvaro isn't just looking for regular cars because he wants to impress his girl friend. He is looking for convenient ways to book a luxury car and rent them without having to search or take other transportation.

Persona 2

Name: Andrew

Age: 33

Occupation: Software Engineer

Hobby: Reading & Skateboarding

Bio: Andrew is a Software Engineer at Google, who loves learning new things, and to adventure new things in his free time. On the weekend, Andrew spends time working as a chauffer for luxury vehicles. Often times, he spends a lot of time visiting car rental agencies so he can rent luxury vehicles at a reasonable price.

Frustration & Cases: Often times, he runs into a situation where he would have to drop off his rented luxury vehicle late at night. In the late evening, there are no staff that helps people facilitating vehicle check out. One day, he was fined $150 for checking in day late (due to rental car agency closure).

Goal: Andrew is looking for new ways to complete check in and check out procedure. Moreover, Andrew is looking for smooth payment plans where he doesn't have to visit the car rental agency or call them late at night for rental payment.

This project had major changes throughout the project. Initially, this project was targeted toward Indiana University students only for the testing purposes. Therefore, I focused the market plans toward IU students only. Through the user interviews, I learned that many people including both students and non students are facing challenges in home cooking due to accessibility, timing and financial reasons. Although systems like UberEats, DoorDash and GrubHub offers a restaurant delivery, often times people get frustrated on long delivery times, ingredients that they do not want on the food and financial outcomes. In order to make people creative and connected, making food at home, cooking their food without having to worry about accessibilty and finance are important factors and solutions. Throughout the project, I learned how to apply user interview to make a better product for the user. I learned how to analyze current product (i.e GrubHub, DoorDash and UberEats) and pain point on user's story with the current technology.
