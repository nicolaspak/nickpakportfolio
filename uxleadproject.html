OVERVIEW

About the project

Super Purposes website was first launched in 2018 as a career coaching startup company. When this website was first launched, there were no emphasis on User research and design. 2 years later, the website is displayed with the same design and same typography as 2 years ago with no customer attraction. In 2020, many young adults and career seekers are facing difficult time preparing for career opportunities. In September 2020, I stepped in, along with 6 other UX researchers to conduct user research to gather user's experience with job posting sites and hear user’s insights on the website. Our team is currently still in research phase gathering surveys and conducting interviews. Due to the non disclosure agreement, I am not able to provide any details on this project until this project is published publicly. This project is in progress and please send me an email if you have any questions.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

Being the Advocator for the User

When working with the small number of teams, every member came up with the different ideas and recommendations. Although it was difficult for all team members to get tie to the one idea, we learned that what we are designing right now is for the users and not just our team. I learned to be a good compromiser and learned how to understand the overall objective of the project.

Collaboration & Critical Thinking

User experience design and research is much more than the research and design. It is about understanding all people and understanding their behavior. In order to have and learn profound understanding of our goals and users goals, I learned that collarboaration and critical thinking skills are very important when conducting user research.

Prioritization

I learned not to overload too many information to users throughout the project process so far. It is important to highlight and define user's journey early in the process rather than waiting until the later phase.

Handling the feedback

I learned the importance of the user centered design and learned how to handle/manage the feedback from the user. I learned the importance of user pain point and finding ways to approach those issues that user face.
